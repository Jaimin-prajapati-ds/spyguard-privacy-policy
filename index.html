# Privacy Policy for SpyGuard **Effective Date:** December 24, 2024 **Last Updated:** December 24, 2024 ## Introduction SpyGuard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. ## Information Collection and Use **We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** SpyGuard is designed with privacy as a core principle: - **No Data Collection:** We do not collect any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics - **No Cloud Storage:** All app data is stored locally on your device only - **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not share any information with third parties - **No Tracking:** We do not track your activity or behavior ## Data Storage All data generated by SpyGuard is stored exclusively on your device: - **PIN/Password:** Encrypted and stored locally - **Intruder Photos:** Saved only on your device - **App Lock Settings:** Stored in local device storage - **Usage Logs:** Kept locally, never transmitted ## Permissions SpyGuard requires the following permissions to function: ### Required Permissions 1. **Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)** - Purpose: To detect when locked apps are launched - Data: Not collected or stored beyond immediate app lock functionality 2. **Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)** - Purpose: To show the lock screen over other apps - Data: No data collected 3. **Foreground Service** - Purpose: To monitor app launches in the background - Data: No data collected ### Optional Permissions 4. **Camera (CAMERA)** - Purpose: To capture intruder selfies when wrong PIN is entered - Data: Photos stored locally only, never uploaded - Note: This permission is optional and can be denied 5. **Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)** - Purpose: To show break-in alerts - Data: No data collected ## Third-Party Services ### Advertising SpyGuard displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad serving purposes. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles data. **What AdMob May Collect:** - Device identifiers - IP address - Ad interaction data **Note:** We do not have access to or control over the data collected by AdMob. ## Children's Privacy SpyGuard does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children. ## Data Security - All sensitive data (PIN, passwords) is encrypted using industry-standard encryption - Data is stored in secure, sandboxed app storage - No data is transmitted over the internet (except AdMob ad requests) ## Your Rights Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to: - Access - Modify - Delete - Export All app data can be deleted by uninstalling the application. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. ## Data Retention - **App Settings:** Retained until app is uninstalled - **Intruder Photos:** Retained until manually deleted by user or app is uninstalled - **PIN/Passwords:** Retained until changed or app is uninstalled All data is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled. ## International Users SpyGuard does not transfer any data internationally as all data is stored locally on your device. ## Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: - **Email:** support@spyguard.app - **GitHub:** https://github.com/Jaimin-prajapati-ds ## Consent By using SpyGuard, you consent to this Privacy Policy. ## Summary **In simple terms:** - We don't collect your data - Everything stays on your phone - We don't send anything to servers - Only AdMob (for ads) may collect standard advertising data - You can delete everything by uninstalling the app --- **SpyGuard - Your Privacy, Your Control** Last Updated: December 24, 2024
